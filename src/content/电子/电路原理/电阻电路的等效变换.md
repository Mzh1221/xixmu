## 电阻串并联

串联等效电阻 $\displaystyle R_{eq}=R_{1}+R_{2}+R_{3}+\dots+R_{n}$
串联分压公式 $\displaystyle u_{k}=R_{k}i=R_{k}\frac{u}{R_{eq}}=u\frac{{R}_{k}}{R_{eq}}$ 
并联等效电导 $\displaystyle G_{eq}=G_{1}+G_{2}+G_{3}+\dots+G_{n}$
并联分流公式 $\displaystyle i_{k}=G_{k}u=G_{k}\frac{i}{G_{eq}}=i\frac{G_{k}}{G_{eq}}$
对于二电阻电路的串联分压和并联分流，串联是自己分总，并联是对方分总

## 星型结构和三角型结构

Y-> $\Delta$ $\displaystyle \left\{ \begin{array}{l} R _ {1 2} = \frac {R _ {1} R _ {2} + R _ {2} R _ {3} + R _ {3} R _ {1}}{R _ {3}} \\ R _ {2 3} = \frac {R _ {1} R _ {2} + R _ {2} R _ {3} + R _ {3} R _ {1}}{R _ {1}} \\ R _ {3 1} = \frac {R _ {1} R _ {2} + R _ {2} R _ {3} + R _ {3} R _ {1}}{R _ {2}} \end{array} \right.$ 就是上面两两相乘除以对面
$\Delta$->Y $\displaystyle \left\{ \begin{array}{l} R _ {1} = \frac {R _ {1 2} R _ {3 1}}{R _ {1 2} + R _ {2 3} + R _ {3 1}} \\ R _ {2} = \frac {R _ {2 3} R _ {1 2}}{R _ {1 2} + R _ {2 3} + R _ {3 1}} \\ R _ {3} = \frac {R _ {3 1} R _ {2 3}}{R _ {1 2} + R _ {2 3} + R _ {3 1}} \end{array} \right.$ 就是和自己相连的两个相乘除以三角形三边
当三个电阻相等时，$\displaystyle R_{\Delta}=3R_{Y}$
